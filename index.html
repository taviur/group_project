Study More

You may be addicted to learning, which is great (you nerd! no really, learning is great) so maybe you're thinking about taking a Masters or a PhD and wondering how the fuck that whole ting even works, because lord knows we had no bloody clue.


First off, we'll define post-study study as 'taught courses' and 'research courses' because as we struggled to find out, not all master programmes are made the same.

Taught Courses


A taught course means, well, you'll be taught more things! You can take a Master of Arts (MA), Master of Science (MSc) or Master of Business Administration (MBA).


Research Courses


A research course means... you'll be researching stuff! You can take a Masters research course or a PhD.
